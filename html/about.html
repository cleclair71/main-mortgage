About Dora

Apply Now

Dora is a seasoned professional in the mortgage industry with 38 years of experience. She joined The Mortgage Professionals in 2006 as a Licensed Mortgage Agent and has previously worked at a major bank and in the Credit Union system since 1978. Dora is an Accredited Mortgage Professional and is dedicated to providing her clients with honest, friendly, and knowledgeable mortgage advice.

Buying a home is one of the most important financial decisions your will make, so using a professional in the Mortgage Industry is in your best interest. As Dora works for you, she will work hard to find the best products, mortgage services to best suit your individual needs.

Whether a first-time buyer, a buyer of a cottage, rental, or second home, and most of all renewing/refinancing your present mortgage, she will put her expertise to work for you. The mortgage process is complicated – please feel free to call/email/text 24/7, to arrange an appointment to discuss your options.

About the Mortgage Professionals

Read More

The Mortgage Professionals began in 1989 in the founder’s garage. Locally owned and operated for almost thirty years, the company has grown to 30+ staff across 5 offices from Kingston to Gananoque.

The Mortgage Professionals are Eastern Ontario’s largest mortgage brokerage, processing over $300m of mortgage volume annually. We are also Kingston’s most recognized mortgage brokerage, having been nominated several times nationally as Mortgage Brokerage of the Year, as Employer of Choice and having won National Mortgage Brokerage of the Year. Our Brokers have been recognized nationally as Top 20 Mortgage Brokers in Canada, locally as Kingston This Week’s Favourite Mortgage Brokers and even in the Canadian Mortgage Hall of Fame.
